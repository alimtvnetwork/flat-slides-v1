**Type:** `DatabaseDiagramSlide`

**Narrow idea:** Three entities link the order graph.

## Why it is narrow
One schema concept: how `USERS`, `ORDERS`, and `ORDER_ITEMS` relate. No columns, no indexes, no auth model. Those belong on later slides.

## Capsules
None. The diagram is the content; a single caption line names the shape.

- Transition: `FadeIn` (the diagram should appear quietly; no push/slide drama).
- Text animation: `FadeIn` for caption.
- Reduced motion: identical (no auto-pan, no auto-zoom).
